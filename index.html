The Diagnosis

Three failure points hiding in your GTM

Activity without intellectual influence is just noise. Here’s where it breaks down.

The Frame Trap

81% of B2B buyers have already chosen a winner before they talk to sales. If your sellers walk into that conversation and agree with the buyer’s existing worldview, they’re not selling — they’re confirming a decision that was made without them.

Invisible to Stakeholders

8–12 stakeholders influence every B2B deal. The average seller is connected to 2–3 of them. That’s not a pipeline problem — it’s an invisibility problem. If the buying group doesn’t know you exist, no amount of outreach will create a winnable deal.

Measuring the Wrong Things

Pipeline volume. Number of meetings. Proposals sent. Your team hits every activity metric while the win rate stays flat. The cost: 12–18 months of GTM effort optimised for a number that was never going to close.
