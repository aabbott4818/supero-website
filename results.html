Data & Identity · Data Axle

Building focus and pipeline strength across BDR, Enterprise, and Client Services

At Data Axle, the challenge wasn’t a lack of opportunity — it was focus. New business and expansion potential existed across hundreds of accounts, but there was no shared view of where future pipeline should really come from.

We used Opportunity Forecast and COS to create a “funnel before the funnel” for BDRs, enterprise sellers, and client services. This gave every team a clear line of sight on which high-value accounts to focus on, where to deepen relationships, and when prospecting urgency needed to spike.

The result was a more disciplined approach to opportunity creation and expansion — with GTM teams spending more time in the right accounts, having more meaningful commercial conversations, and building pipeline that was actually winnable.

Attribution & Measurement · Exactag

From zombie pipeline to a commercially intelligent GTM model

When we started working together, Exactag had more than €5M in pipeline — and a 6% win rate. The problem wasn’t activity. It was a paradigm problem: sellers were reacting to how buyers already thought about attribution, instead of leading the conversation.

We identified four value drivers — measurement integrity, modelling rigour, insight activation, and governance — and built a teaching POV around decision confidence in marketing investment. We then encoded that logic into account plans, outreach, and Deal Maker, and used AI to generate commercially intelligent account plans in under an hour.

The result was a GTM operating model that consistently reframed buyer thinking, improved win probabilities, and made every deal review an opportunity to refine the system.

Global Travel · Royal Caribbean International

Equipping 200+ international sales reps with a conversation operating system

Royal Caribbean wanted a way to modernise how its international sales team showed up in market — without losing the authenticity and personality that makes great sellers great. The goal was global scale, not a one-off training.

We designed and rolled out a COS-based programme across more than 200 sales professionals. It combined foundational skills, LMS-based learning journeys, onsite workshops, and ongoing coaching — all aligned to the brand’s voice and messaging.

The work connected outreach strategies with brand storytelling, helping sellers build stronger relationships with trade partners and customers, show up more consistently online, and anchor conversations in value rather than volume.
