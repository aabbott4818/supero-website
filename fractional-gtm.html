How Fractional GTM Works

Three phases, one operating system

We combine COS, 30 years of B2B experience, and AI to design and run a GTM system that wins the confidence battle in every deal.

Phase 01

Diagnose

We run a GTM diagnostic: pipeline quality, win rates, deal reviews, stakeholder coverage, conversation quality, and how your current systems support (or block) all of this.

Phase 02

Architect

We design your Conversation Operating System: value drivers, buyer journeys, conversation plays, relationship metrics, and qualification scorecards grounded in your reality.

Phase 03

Operationalise

We embed that system into the tools your teams already use — CRM, outreach, LinkedIn, enablement — and build AI components so strategic thinking is available on demand.

What Working With Us Looks Like

Coaching conversations plus GTM design

Every engagement has two layers: live coaching in real deals, and operating system design that turns those insights into repeatable plays.

Coaching Layer

Real deals, real shifts

We work live on your pipeline — from experienced sales veterans to new sellers. Sessions focus on moving from following the buyer’s process to shaping it, and from activity to relationship strength.

Framework Layer

COS as the backbone

We codify patterns from those sessions into your COS: triggers, questions, plays, and narratives that capture what good looks like for your team.

System & Integration Layer

In stride, not in slides

We map the framework into your systems so intelligence shows up where people work — in account views, call prep, outreach sequences, and deal reviews. COS integrates with your CRM, enablement stack, and AI tools so the operating system runs in the background, every day.

Opportunity Forecast

The spotlight before the funnel

Opportunity Forecast is the fastest way to bring discipline and focus to your GTM — a fundamental component of the operating system that shows where real opportunity will come from.

See future pipeline clearly

We build a forward-looking view that shows which accounts must move in the next 30/60/90 days to protect your number — and where you’re relying on wishful thinking.

Refine ICP & targeting

Where needed, we refine your ICP, then use COS to target and prioritise accounts based on return on effort — not just logo lust. Sellers see which accounts are worth the work.

Urgency with purpose

Opportunity Forecast brings urgency to prospecting by making it obvious when prospecting must spike — and what “enough” looks like to build a winnable pipeline.

I first built this view in 2008 to know when to prioritise prospecting. It helped me never miss quota in almost 20 years — doubling quota as a new rep, hitting target in the first six months in a new region (Asia) with no network, and building a $22M ARR business in four years in the Middle East. Today, we operationalise Opportunity Forecast inside COS so entire teams can benefit from the same discipline.
